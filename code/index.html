Our Parish

Our parish St Behanans Indian Orthodox Church, North Wales, is under the UK-Europe and Africa Diocese of the Indian Orthodox Syrian Church under the spiritual leadership of H.H. Baselios Marthoma Mathews III. Our parish has close to registered families. Total members including family members are north of 70.Our parish members are from Llandudno, Colwyn Bay, Flint, Ruthin, Rhyl, Abergele, Chester and Wrexham.
